### **Specific policy/industry dynamics public opinion analysis report template**

- **1.0 Summary: Core impact of policies/dynamics and public opinion response**
- 1.1 Interpretation of core content
- 1.2 Core viewpoints in the public opinion field
- 1.3 Prediction of key impacts
- **2.0 Policy/Dynamic Background and Communication Analysis**
- 2.1 Release background and interpretation
- 2.2 Development and fermentation timeline
- 2.3 Analysis of propagated sound volume
- 2.4 Authoritative interpretation and media focus
- **3.0 Public Attitudes and Social Sentiment**
- 3.1 Distribution of public opinion and sentiment
- 3.2 Focus on the viewpoints of all parties
- **4.0 Analysis of potential impacts, opportunities and challenges**
- 4.1 Impact on industry structure
- 4.2 Impact on the enterprise (opportunities and challenges)
- 4.3 Impact on related industries
- **5.0 Industry Benchmark Cases and Responses**
- 5.1 Response from competitors/leading companies
- 5.2 Perspectives of industry associations/organizations
- **6.0 Conclusion and response suggestions**
- 6.1 Trend analysis and judgment
- 6.2 Strategic response suggestions
- 6.3 Public relations communication suggestions
